## Agenda

Reclaim lost Dwarven strongholds, eliminate enemy lieutenants, restore the strength and prominence of the Dwarves in Eriador. Balin is particularly interested in Moria (Khazad-dûm), where his father was slain.

---

## Encountering Balin

[[TOR_Core_Rules.pdf#page=217|Core Rules p.213]]

Balin is unusually woodcrafty for a Dwarf — skilled at tracking, fire-making, and pathfinding. He often journeys with Gandalf, and visits the Shire to see Bilbo. He travels between Erebor and the Blue Mountains regularly.

You might meet him at:
- A small inn between Erebor and the Blue Mountains
- Camping in the wild near the road
- Passing through Bree or the Shire

He is generous, kind-spirited, and easily the wisest of those who reclaimed the Lonely Mountain. He may accompany the Company for a stretch if their purposes align.

---

## Balin as a Patron

Unlike most Dwarves, Balin genuinely takes an interest in other folk — especially Hobbits. He hates Orcs and seeks to drive them back wherever he can. He encourages allies to scout Dwarven-fallen strongholds and discover what lurks therein, while counselling patience over rash action.

**What he might ask of the Company:**
- Scout locations associated with lost Dwarven holds
- Eliminate a dangerous enemy lieutenant
- Gather intelligence about the state of Moria or other fallen Dwarven sites
