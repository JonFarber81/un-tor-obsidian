### Description
Rowan is a tall, lean woman in her middle years with keen gray eyes and a calm, steady presence. Her dark hair is streaked with silver, and she moves with quiet confidence, her bow always within reach. Unlike [[Haleth]], Rowan believes in judging each encounter on its own merits, having seen good come from unlikely sources during her long years watching the roads. While cautious, she shows a measured warmth to newcomers, seeing potential allies where others see only risk.


### Distinctive Traits
- **Measured:** Careful and thoughtful, Rowan weighs her words and listens before passing judgment.
- **Hopeful:** Though tempered by experience, she still believes that some good can be found even in dark times.
