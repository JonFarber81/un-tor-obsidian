A Patron is an influential individual who has taken an interest in the Company. Each provides a **Fellowship Bonus** and a unique **Advantage** tied to spending Fellowship points. A Company designates one as their **main Patron** during a Fellowship Phase via the Meet Patron undertaking.

## How Patrons Work

- The Company **starts the game with one Patron** (add more during play)
- Only **one Patron** may be designated the main Patron at a time
- Only the **main Patron's Fellowship Bonus and Advantage** apply
- To designate or change Patron: choose the **Meet Patron** undertaking during a Fellowship Phase, while in the same location as that Patron

> The Favoured Callings of a Patron indicate what they value — and what they're likely to ask of the Company in return for support.
