## Agenda

Recover lost lore and lost things. Bilbo is always gathering rumours, tidings, and bits of knowledge for his personal records — he is planning to turn his diary into a real book. He is especially interested in maps and geographical lore, which he uses to amend his own charts.

---

## Encountering Bilbo

[[TOR_Core_Rules.pdf#page=217|Core Rules p.213]]

In the year 2965, Bilbo is 75 — vigorous, curious, and a generous host. He lives at Bag End and is most likely to be found there, probably having tea or standing at the door with a pipe. He sometimes wanders as far as Buckland or Michel Delving, and in the company of Dwarves he may travel further still.

If encountered outside the Shire, Bilbo may carry **Sting**, his Elf-dagger — though he rarely needs it. He has an uncanny ability to disappear almost instantly when threatened.

> [!warning] Bilbo's Magic Ring
> Bilbo possesses a magic ring found under the Misty Mountains — it renders him invisible when worn. He keeps it on a fine silver chain in his waistcoat pocket and habitually touches it for reassurance. After twenty years, it is slowly developing a growing hold over his mind. He does not speak of it.

## Bilbo as a Patron

Bilbo knows the world outside the Shire well and is aware of its dangers. He keeps himself informed via travellers, letters, and his network of acquaintances. He is happy to:
- Finance expeditions
- Arrange hospitality
- Share maps and geographical knowledge
- Provide leads on lost things or lore

He asks only for a good story and perhaps a souvenir or keepsake in return.

**What he might ask of the Company:**
- Recover a piece of lost lore, a map, or a forgotten artefact
- Bring him news from the wider world
- Investigate something suspicious that might threaten the Shire
