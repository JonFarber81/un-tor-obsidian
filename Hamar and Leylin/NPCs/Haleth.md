### Description
Haleth is a weathered man in his late fifties, with gray streaks through his once-dark hair and a long, deep scar running across his left cheek. His face is sharp and hard, his eyes deeply set and often narrowed in suspicion. A heavy, travel-worn cloak hangs from his shoulders, and he carries a long, worn spear that he wields with practiced ease. Haleth has spent years surviving in the wilds, and hardship has made him deeply distrustful of outsiders. To him, every stranger is a potential threat until proven otherwise.

### Distinctive Traits
- **Suspicious:** Rarely trusts words alone; he judges others by their actions and is slow to accept help.
- **Grim:** Speaks little and expects the worst from any situation, always preparing for betrayal or disaster.
