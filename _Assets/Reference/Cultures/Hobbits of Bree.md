## Character Creation Stats
> [[TOR_Core_Rules.pdf#page=44|Core Rules p.40–42]]

> [!Rolls] Attribute Array
> Assign these three values freely to **Strength / Heart / Wits**:
> **2 · 6 · 6** *(total 14)*
>
> *(Same array as Hobbits of the Shire; Bree-Hobbits differ in cultural virtues and blessing — see rulebook)*

> [!combat] Derived Stats
> - **Endurance** = **18 + Strength** *(e.g. STR 2 → Endurance 20)*
> - **Hope** = **10 + Heart** *(e.g. HRT 6 → Hope 16)*
> - **Parry** = 10 + primary weapon skill + shield bonus
> - **Standard of Living:** Common

> [!Council] Favoured Skills *(start with 1 free rank)*
> - **Awareness** (Strength)
> - **Stealth** (Wits)
> - **Insight** (Heart)

> [!Council] Cultural Virtues *(cost 1 AP each — [[TOR_Core_Rules.pdf#page=44|p.40]])*
> | Virtue | Brief Effect |
> |--------|-------------|
> | *(Blessing)* | See embedded PDF above |
> | Friends in Bree | Courtesy and Insight rolls in Bree-land are Favoured |
> | Well-met Stranger | When meeting a new NPC, gain (1d) on first Courtesy roll |
> | Crossroads of the West | Know rumours and roads of Eriador; Explore rolls Favoured |
> | Small but Brave | Once per combat, resist a Fear test automatically |
> | Heirloom | Start with a useful passed-down item |

> [!combat] Gear Restrictions — HALFLINGS
> Cannot use: **Great Shield**
> Available weapons: Axe · Bow · Club · Cudgel · Dagger · Short Sword · Short Spear · Spear
