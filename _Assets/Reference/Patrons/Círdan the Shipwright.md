## Agenda

Rekindle hope among the Free Peoples. Preserve the lore of the Ages. Ensure that all who desire to leave Middle-earth can do so safely from the Grey Havens. His memory stretches back before the Sun and Moon.

## Encountering Círdan

[[TOR_Core_Rules.pdf#page=219|Core Rules p.216]]

Círdan dwells at Mithlond and rarely ventures forth, but all who come in peace are welcome. He is perhaps the **most ancient Elf still living in Middle-earth**, having witnessed three ages of the world. He was keeper of the Elven Ring Narya (the Ring of Fire) until he gave it to Gandalf.

In person, Círdan is very tall with a long silver beard — a rarity among Elves. He is gracious and hospitable. His keen, bright eyes carry a depth of memory that goes back before the light of Sun and Moon.

His herald and envoy, **Galdor of the Havens**, often travels across Middle-earth in his name.

> [!lore] The Elendil Stone
> Círdan and the Elves of Lindon guard one of the seven *palantíri* (seeing-stones) brought from Númenor. Called the Elendil Stone, it is locked in the Tower of Elostirion in the Tower Hills. Whether it still reveals visions is known only to a few of the Wise.

---

## Círdan as a Patron

The Grey Havens are strategically vital — the Five Wizards landed there, and many great fleets armed for the defeat of Sauron found harbour there. Círdan ensures Mithlond remains the last refuge for those who wish to leave Middle-earth.

His knowledge rivals Elrond's. The Lord of the Havens and Imladris frequently counsel each other.

**What he might ask of the Company:**
- Carry messages or items between Mithlond and other Wise
- Investigate a threat along the road between Eriador and the Havens
- Preserve or recover a piece of ancient lore
