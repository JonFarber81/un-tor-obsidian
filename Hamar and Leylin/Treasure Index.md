## Marvelous Artifacts

### Star-Eye Pendant  
A delicate silver amulet in the shape of an open eye, set with a pale blue gem that faintly glows under moonlight. Forged in forgotten Eregion and said to be blessed by Elbereth herself, the pendant sharpens the senses of its wearer.

**Effect:** The bearer gains *(2d)* on **Awareness** rolls. Alternatively, the bearer may spend a point of Hope to automatically succeed on an **Awareness** roll (Magical Success).

**Whispers say** the pendant grants momentary flashes of foresight, letting the wearer glimpse hidden threats before they emerge.

### Voice of the Deep Stone  
This finely wrought torc of blackened steel and gold bears runes of Khuzdul etched along its length, humming faintly when worn beneath the mountains. Crafted in ancient Khazad-dûm, it is said to echo the authority of the mountain lords of old.

**Effect:** The bearer gains *(2d)* on **Awe** rolls. Alternatively, the bearer may spend a point of Hope to automatically succeed on an **Awe** roll (Magical Success).

**Dwarves speak in hushed tones** of how the voice of the wearer seems to carry deeper and farther, commanding respect even from those who do not know their name.

### Hearth-Warm Token  
A small, carved wooden charm shaped like a cozy hobbit-hole door, warm to the touch and faintly smelling of pipe-weed and baking bread. Passed down through generations of a quiet Shire family, it carries the lingering comfort of home and hearth.

**Effect:** The bearer gains *(2d)* on **Enhearten** rolls. Alternatively, the bearer may spend a point of Hope to automatically succeed on an **Enhearten** roll (Magical Success).

**Those near the bearer** often feel steadier in dark places, as if a memory of crackling fires and friendly laughter steadies their hearts.

## Famous Weapons and Armor

### Willowsting  
*Famous Weapon – Short Sword*
Forged in the waning days of the North Kingdom, Willowsting is a finely balanced short sword with a blade of pale steel and a guard shaped like curling willow branches. Light and swift, it was made for a ranger of small stature who once patrolled the hidden paths of the Trollshaws.

Lost during a desperate skirmish with trolls near the ruins of Rhudaur, the sword lay hidden beneath moss and stone for centuries, its edge untouched by time. Elvish script on the blade reads: *“Swift as wind through the trees, silent as shadow.”*

**Load:** 0
**Damage:** 3  
**Injury:** 16  
**Bane:** Orcs
**Qualities:**  
- [ ] **Cunning Make:** Reduce Load by 1.  
- [ ] **Keen:** Piercing Blow on a roll of 9+.  
- [ ] **Fell:** Add 2 to injury rating
