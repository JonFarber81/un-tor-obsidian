### Description
Beran is a wiry young man in his early twenties, with unruly dark hair and a face still unmarked by the deep lines of hardship his older companions bear. His brown eyes are quick and restless, constantly scanning the surroundings even when he’s trying to appear calm. He wears a simple, travel-worn cloak and carries a short sword and a hunting bow—tools of necessity rather than pride. Though clearly inexperienced compared to the others, there’s a spark of determination in him, as if he’s eager to prove his worth among the Troll Watchers.

### Distinctive Traits
- **Alert:** Always wary of danger, Beran notices small movements and subtle changes in the environment, sometimes even before his elders do.
- **Eager:** Young and earnest, Beran is eager to make a name for himself and often speaks or acts before thinking things fully through.

Beran seems more open and approachable than the older Troll Watchers, though still bound by the group's secretive caution.
