### Bio
Haldar is a veteran Ranger of the North, one of Gilraen's most trusted patrol captains in the North Downs. He served under Arador, Gilraen's father-in-law, and has walked the old roads of Arnor for more than thirty years. He is aging now — slower than he was, and not inclined to admit it — but his knowledge of the terrain and the Enemy's patterns is unmatched among the northern patrols.

He is stubborn, proud, and deeply uncomfortable with being rescued. He will repay a debt, but he will not enjoy owing one.

### Distinctive Features
- **Stubborn:** Haldar will not ask for help and will resist accepting it. Argue a point with him through demonstrated competence, not words.
- **Forthright:** He does not flatter, soften, or spare feelings. What he tells you is what he believes to be true.
- **Wary:** Thirty years of ranging has made him slow to trust newcomers, even those vouched for by Gilraen.

### Relationship with Aldric
Met in Phase [[1.1 AP - The Missing Watch]] — rescued from Orc captivity at Greenwatch Post. Owes Aldric his life, which sits uneasily with him. Will be a reliable (if gruff) source of intelligence on Orc movements in the North Downs going forward.

### Known Information
- Knows the patrol routes of the North Downs in detail
- Witnessed Orc scouts mapping Ranger patrol patterns near Greenwatch (if Telling Table roll was YES in Phase 1.1)
- Has contacts among Ranger captains operating near Annúminas and the Weather Hills
